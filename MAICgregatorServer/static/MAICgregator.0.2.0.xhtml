MAICgregator 0.2.0

Highlighting the words of today's global university!

Two main new features in this release:

  • MAICgregator now highlights the important words of today's university, words such as "innovation", "business", "corporate", and "technology transfer".
  • MAICgregator also searches for clinical trials in which the university is involved.

Both options are available under "MAICgregator preferences".
