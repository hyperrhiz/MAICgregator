Download MAICgregator

To install MAICgregator, you can simply click on the button below:

MAICgregator only works in Firefox version 3 and above.

You will have to specifically "allow" this site to install the MAICgregator extension. When you click on the "Download MAICgregator" button, a small window will appear in which you will have to click "allow". When the next window pops up, you will be able to click "Install now" after a few second wait. Do so, wait for the extension to download and install, and then restart your browser.
